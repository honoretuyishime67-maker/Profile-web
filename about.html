About Me

Dedicated to transforming education in Rwanda through innovative technology integration, empowering both teachers and students with the digital skills needed for a brighter future.

Background

I am Tuyishime Honore, a passionate educator currently serving as a teacher at Rukara Model School of Sciences and Mathematics, one of Rwanda's leading educational institutions. Born on February 28, 2002, I am driven by a mission to transform education in Rwanda through innovative technology integration. My commitment to excellence in education drives me to continuously expand my knowledge and skills.

Education & Growth

I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Physics Education at Kigali Independent University (ULK), Gisenyi Campus. I completed my teaching qualifications through advanced studies at TTC Matimba (A2 Diploma) and the Primary Teaching Residency Program at TTC De La Salle. This academic journey complements my practical experience and helps me bridge the gap between traditional teaching methods and modern educational technology.

Training & Impact

As an ICT Trainer with PISQUARE, supported by Edify, I work at the intersection of education and technology. I have trained 100+ primary school teachers in digital literacy and ICT integration. I've received specialized training from the Rwanda Education Board (World Bank partnership), University of Rwanda Centre of Excellence, and Microsoft. My mission is to empower teachers and students across Rwanda with the skills necessary to thrive in our increasingly connected world.
